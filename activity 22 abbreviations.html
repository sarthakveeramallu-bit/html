Abbreviations

It is used to mark up an abbreviation or acronym:

cdgl was found in 2020

Blockquote

It is used present content from another source:

Codingal is on mission to inspire all the school kids.

Codingal Team

Multiple Code Lines

For multiple lines of code, use the pre element:

            codingal is on
            mission to inspire
            all the school kids.
